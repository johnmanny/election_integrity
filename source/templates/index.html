INFORMATION

This project demonstrates a voting encryption scheme which uses homomorphic secret sharing and features vote verification using Lagrange polynomials. Each vote is encoded as a constant term in a polynomial with the coefficients of the remaining terms acting as user-defined keys. Those user-defined coefficients allow the voter to send separate terms , all carrying information about the vote they cast, to different designated vote counters and eventually tally their vote.

To participate in a vote, click the 'register to vote' link on the left and generate a key. Once you do that, the server stores it automatically. From there you can go to the 'vote' page and record your vote using your coefficients. After your vote has been cast, the 'verify results' screen will output the outcome of the election based on the submitted and confirmed values by the voters
